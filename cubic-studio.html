Privacy & Data Policy

LAST UPDATED — 2025

Who We Are

Cubic Studio is a professional photography and film production studio. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you make a booking through this app, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

What Data We Collect

When you submit a booking, we collect the following information you provide directly:

Client: First name, last name, mobile number, session date(s) and time slots.
Photographer: Name, mobile number, Instagram handle.
Model: Name, mobile number, Instagram handle.

We do not collect payment details, passwords, location data, or any sensitive personal information through this app.

How We Use Your Data

Your booking data is used solely to:
— Confirm and schedule your studio session
— Notify the studio owner of new bookings
— Contact you regarding your reservation

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party advertisers or marketing services.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Booking data is stored in a Google Sheet accessible only to the studio owner, using Google's secure infrastructure. Data is transmitted via an encrypted HTTPS connection. Google's own privacy policy applies to storage: policies.google.com

How Long We Keep It

Booking records are retained for up to 12 months after your session date for scheduling reference and dispute resolution, then permanently deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us directly through the studio's official communication channels. Requests are processed within 7 business days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out to Cubic Studio directly. By proceeding, you confirm you are 18 years or older or have parental consent.
